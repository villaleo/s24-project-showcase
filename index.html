This summer, our W+IT pathway members focused on front end web development to create websites from scratch, utilizing important tools and software such as HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 4, and JavaScript. All of our websites are being hosted by GitHub Pages.

The front end of a website or web application is the part that users interact with directly. It's also known as the client side and includes everything that users see, hear, or use to interact with a device or software.
